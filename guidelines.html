Rate My GSI

Review Guidelines

When contributing to Rate My GSI, we kindly ask you to follow the following guidelines:

  • Be honest in your reviews. To ensure credibility, it's important to provide truthful feedback that others can rely on when evaluating their course options.
  • Include both pros and cons in your reviews.
  • Focus on the course and your learning experience.
  • Express why the course or GSI didn't align with your learning style in a way that helps others make informed decisions.
  • Avoid writing reviews fueled by anger. Profanity and disrespectful language may result in the removal of your review.
  • Submit only one review per GSI per course.
  • Exercise discretion when reading reviews. Consider multiple perspectives and use online reviews as one of many resources for making academic decisions.

We appreciate your cooperation in maintaining a respectful and helpful environment for all users of Rate My GSI.
